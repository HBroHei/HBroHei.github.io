NSMBW Custom level

Here you will find a lost of custom New Super Mario Bros Wii stages made by me with the Reggie! Level editor. All files are in .arc format. To play them, rename the file to "01-01.arc", and replace the one in the "Stage" folder of the game.
For more information on how to play these levels, search up "NSMBW import custom levels".

Manual Level

The level that is shown in the manual of the game.
Note: this level is based on the Tranditional Chinese Version of the manual.
Difficulty: Easy / Normal

Begin My Kaizo

A level with some Kaizo tricks and difficult jumps.
Note: Some indicators might be inaccurate in this level.
Difficulty: Super Expert
